INFORMATION & COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY

ICT degree is a 4-year Bachelors degree programme, in years three (3) and four (4), goes on to offer three distinct areas of specialization, namely, Networking and Cyber Security, Mobile and Digital Media Development (gaming), and Software Engineering (computer programming). Students have the opportunity to major in one or two of these areas of specialization during their course of study.

After completing the programme graduates will be able to demonstrate the competencies required to function effectively as ICT professionals. Communicate clearly to stakeholders using a variety of methods and approaches,apply good analytical, design, and implementation skills required to formulate and solve technology based problems.Analyse various implementation approaches to distributed domains.Design cloud based services that fulfill the requirements of distributed virtualization.
